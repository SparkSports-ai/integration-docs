Spark is SparkSports' live sports streak game. Players pick outcomes across consecutive live markets, build a multiplier, and cash out before the streak ends.

There are two ways to integrate. Which one applies to you depends on whether your casino connects to Spark directly or through an aggregator platform.

## Two ways to integrate

### Operator integration

You connect your casino backend directly to Spark. You host the wallet and session-validation endpoints we call, and you call our launch endpoint to get a game URL. This is the path for a casino that owns its own wallet and player account system.

### Aggregator integration

Your casino sits behind an aggregator platform such as Upgaming. The platform handles the integration with Spark. You build no wallet or session endpoints. The aggregator calls Spark, and Spark calls the aggregator's wallet.

<Cards>
  <Card title="Aggregator integration" href="/docs/aggregator-integration/overview" description="For platforms like Upgaming" />
</Cards>

If you are not sure which path applies, ask your SparkSports contact. If your casino runs on a platform that already integrates with Spark, you are on the aggregator path and do not build anything in these docs.

## Player experience

Spark loads in an iframe in your lobby. The browser only gets a short-lived launch URL. Wallet credentials stay on your backend (operator path) or on the aggregator (aggregator path).

## Getting set up

Ask your SparkSports contact for staging credentials, callback configuration, and go-live scheduling.
