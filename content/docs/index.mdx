**Spark** is SparkSports' live sports streak game. Players pick outcomes across consecutive live markets, build a multiplier, and cash out before their streak ends.

This guide explains how casino operators integrate Spark into their platform.

## What you build

Integration has three parts:

1. **Game launch** — Your backend requests a game URL when a player opens Spark.
2. **Session validation** — SparkSports validates the player session with your backend before loading the game.
3. **Wallet callbacks** — SparkSports debits and credits player balances through synchronous HTTP calls to your API.

There is no SDK and no webhook delivery. All communication is **synchronous HTTP over TLS**.

## Player experience

Spark runs in an **iframe** inside your casino lobby. Your backend never exposes wallet credentials to the browser — only a short-lived launch URL.
